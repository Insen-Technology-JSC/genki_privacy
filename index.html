Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

We may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to Usage Data.

Health Data

We collect certain health-related information accessed via your device's health services, including:

  • Steps count
  • Heart rate
  • Body temperature
  • Oxygen saturation (SpO₂)
  • Respiratory rate
  • Blood pressure
  • Sleep data

This data is collected to support the Early Warning System (EWS) within the app, which monitors these vital signs to detect early signs of health deterioration, provide health insights, track progress, and offer personalized recommendations. The EWS is designed to enhance user safety by identifying potential health risks based on variations in these physiological measurements.

  • Storage: Your health data is stored on our secure servers for app functionality, backup, and syncing across devices.
  • Analytics: We may use de-identified or aggregated health data for analytics to improve app performance and features.
  • No Advertising/Insurance Sharing: We do not share your identifiable health data for advertising or insurance purposes.
